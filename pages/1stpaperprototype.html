My first Paper Prototype

We were given a class assignment to form groups of 4 and design a game for a few hours, after we were made to make that game into a paper prototype. my group and i decided to make a action strategy game where you have to position yourself in battle and attack from certain ranges to kill all enemies on the board.


Paper Prototype Full Map


Info on Image
  • The image right next to this info box was taken when everyone got to play test their paper prototypes. The one thing we learned emediatly was that the rules were not written down and we had to make the it as fast as possible. But also that the game was fun and not to complicated for players to enjoy themselfs.


Movement

The movement was pretty simple, each character had a certain amount of walking distance that they could move, just think of it like a chess board. but the archer could move further than the swordsmen.

Level System

When making the levels we decided to have it where the player can make the levels themselves, like a puzzle where you have to slide the correct pieces into the correct spots, but instead it would be a dungeon map.



Dice
Dice Rolls

Dice rolls would be used to determine the multiplication of each attack and how much damage it did.

RPG
RPG Combat System

A RPG combat system of stats and attacks was used, where the player could make the best of his pieces by choosing the correct characters.

Enemies
Enchanter & Mellee

There were 2 enemies in the paper prototype, an enchanter which boosted enemies. and a melee enemy.



Items

At the start of each level the player would be able to choose 3 items to bring with him into the level, making it so that you have to strategise before even entering the level.

Rules

The rules were simple, no moving diagly, and attacking was done by hitting a damage board and then rolling the dice for random extra damage.

Damage Calculation

The damage was also Calculated by hitting a board which added either 1, 3, or 5 extra damage to each attack, making so the player has to pay attention if he want to deal extra damage.
