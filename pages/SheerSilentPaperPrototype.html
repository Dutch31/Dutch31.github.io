Sheer Silent Paper Prototype

This was an exam paper which i had to make a paper prototype within a few hours and present it to my teacher, it went well and i learned how to faster and better work with paper and include game mechanics in my final result, but it was also esential to be flexible and change a few mechanics when i saw that they did not work.


Paper Prototype Full Map


Info on Image
  • The map you can see to your right was actually made in an hour, we had limited time as this was a part of my national exam for my class game design. i learned that i could use half bent ice cream sticks to form walls and make obstacles, whilst also making it where its clear how the enemy can move by using a stick on the head.


Combat System

The combat is a quick time event where the player can slow down time and hit the right buttons when using the future sight ability.

Movement

The movement is not tile based and the player is free to roam around and get a better feel for their surroundings as they sneak their way past security.



Bar

The player will have 2 bars under their name, a health bar and an ability bar showing how much time they have left to use their ability.

Ability

The ability allows you to sneak past certain enemies by using future sight and seeing where the enemy will go, where it will adventure to.

Stealth

The game also relies on stealth and rewards players for chosing a slower aproach to sneak around buildings and enemies.
