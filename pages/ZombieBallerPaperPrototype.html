Zombie Baller

Paper Prototype

Zombie baller Paper Prototype

This was our first prototype of the core gameplay loop, i think it went well and it worked out to be a good exercize to get better at making and designing paper prototypes escpecially when they are top down.


Paper Prototype Video


Info on video
  • The video shows everything done in 1 hour of time. how me and our group of 6 made a game prototype as fast as we could, making enemies, weapons and much more. if you do go and watch the video in the Zombie baller Game Development page, youll see that not every feature made it to the final product. That is because of our lack in time and because of our bugs that we found in our game.


Player

The player was controlled by a person, which used a stick to rotate and face enemies as he decided to shoot run or kill his enemies.

Game Over

The game over sign was a quickly made piece of paper that was stuck on a stick, it was good and was used efficiently to quicly put in frame when the player died.



Enemies

The enemies were in different sizes and in different strenghts, one was fast, whilst the other was strong and we decided to even have a boss in the paper prototype. But couldnt get the boss in the game due to lack of time.

Map

The map was big but also weirldy drawn because we were going as fast as possible and only had an hour to make this entire paper prototype.

Boost

We decided to add boosts because we felt like the core gameplay was lacking excitement and randomness, so we added randomly located boost pannels to make the player twice as fast.
