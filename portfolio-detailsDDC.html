Deep Depth's Crypt Page

This is a Text based adventure game, where you decide and fight your way out.


Shop System

The shop system is a big system in this game that uses the coins the player collects to buy upgrades for your weapons, armor, and to up the difficulty for higher rewards. Whilst also being able to stock up on potions.

Difficulty

In the shop you are able to boost difficulty for bigger coin rewards but also stronger enemies.



Choice's of Adventure

This game also allows its players to make the decision of what path to follow and how to advance in the game to make a playthrought just that more unique.

Combat Tutorial

The combat tutorial teaches you how to fight and how to play the game, whilst also getting some coins in return.

Home Base

Home base is where you will have the option to either explore, to kill enemies and go out to hunt, or open the shop and try to improve your weapon and armor.

Boss Battles

The boss battles would occur after 9 fights with random monsters and would be named monsters much stronger than the rest.
