Dungeon Divers Page

This project was made in 20 hours since thats all the class time we were given. Me and my partner for this project wanted to make a multiplayer game. Since we knew we didnt have a lot of time it was decided to make it a 2D dungeon crawler where you and your friends could fight enemies and go through dungeons.


Locked Chest

I devided to add locked chests to the game to give an encentive to get the red key from within the dungeon. This also gives the player the chance to get more points in the form of coins. Of course there are also normal chests which dont need a key to be opened by the player.

Keys/Inventory

I made an inventory system for managing the keys and to show them in the ui when the player has collected them. The green key can be used to open the green door and the red key can be used to open the red door.


Locked Door

There are doors that are unlocked and can be opened at any time. But the green door can only be opened by the green key to make the player explore the dungeon to find the green key.

Enemies

Slime Behavior

The slime is a more common enemy found within the dungeon. Its behavior had been coded to move towards the player and then do a leaping attack to the direction of the player. But I also made it where the slime cant see throught colliders with the layer "Wall".

Enemies

Enemies both have different behavior because the slime is a regular common enemy whilst the goblin is a boss monster at the end of the dungeon.

Goblin Boss

This Goblin is the boss at the end of the game. I made it where the goblin can only attack when he is inside the trigger called BossRoom to make sure he doesnt leave it. How the behavior works is simple, the goblin moves towards the player and does a slash attack.

Maps

Hub

The Hub was made for the starting player to have a location to start the multiplayer lobby where their friends could join and then head to the dungeons together. There was also the chance of making a shop here with the coins that you collected in the dungeon but we ran out of time for that.

Center Dungeon

The Center area of the dungeon is where the player spawns and is where most of the content is. in the dungeon you could find coins, traps, enemies, keys, stair cases heading to different areas and much more.

Full Dungeon

This is the full dungeon that wasnt visible in the center dungeon card. Here you can see where each staircase leads to and what can be obtained. The top left staircase leads to the left section and the left section leads to the bottom right staircase. Whilst the top right staircase in the center dungeon leads to the boss room in the top right. You need a key to use this staircase because of the green door.
