Mini-GameJam Kidnap Cats Page

This game was made in a very rushed way. It was a game jam which we had to finish in 3 days. The theme was kidnapping cats.

Rank

The rank at the end of the game is determinined by the amount of points the player gathered.

Map

The map was made by me and i designed it like an island map where the cats were spawning in random positions on the map.

Points

You could collect points by kidnapping cats in the cat pen. Each cat would give you 10 points. there is a time limit so it would be very difficult to get a lot of points.
