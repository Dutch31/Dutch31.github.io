Office Ops Page

This was a school project i had 7 weeks the time for and there were a few requirements that we learned about for the first time, like for example the use of scriptable objects and singletons, but also interactables. It was a good learning experience and i managed to finish the game in time.

Searching

In the game there is a searching area where the player has to look for the key in an office. The player can look in boxes, drawers, or lockers.

Weapon Switching

You can switch between a yellow pistol and a purple rifle which have different fire rates, damage, mag size. They are both managed by a scriptable object.

Gun Fight

At the end of the searching area there is a shootout with 2 enemy AI. They use 4 waypoints to circle around the area. When they see the player and lose sight of the player they start searching at the last seen position.
