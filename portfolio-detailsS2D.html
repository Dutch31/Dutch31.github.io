Shrink2D Page

This is a 2D platformer made in Unreal Engine for the intake exam at Breda University of Applied Sciences. I passed and wanted to showcase the project that helped me gain admission to my dream university.


Checkpoint System

The checkpoint system allows the player to respawn after dying, ensuring they do not lose too much progress.

Changing Size

The main concept of the game was changing the playerâ€™s size, and I centered many core gameplay mechanics around this feature. This allowed players to traverse the level in different ways depending on their size.



Size Based Jumppad

The springs were designed to boost the player higher depending on their size: a heavier player received a stronger boost, while a smaller player was boosted less.






Cloud Platforms

Cloud platforms could only be jumped on if the player was small enough; otherwise, the player would fall through them.


Pipes

The pipes were designed to keep the player aware of their surroundings and encourage them to explore every nook and cranny. Additionally, pipes come in different sizes, allowing the player to enter only those matching their current size.



End level Exit

The level exit was designed to provide a clear way to finish the level and advance to the next stage.

Concept

The concept was to encourage the player to explore while also shifting their perspective on which size is best suited for each situation.

There were many interactions based on the playerâ€™s size, affecting their jump height, strength, and accessibility within the game world.
