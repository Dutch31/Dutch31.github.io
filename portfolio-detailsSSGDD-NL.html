Quick Story Summary

A player must rescue their kidnapped family from a hostile organization. The player fights through enemy bases, gathers intel, infiltrates the organization's headquarters, defeats the boss, and saves their family.

USP

In this stealth game, the main feature is the player's ability to see a glimpse of the future, allowing them to predict enemy movement and plan their attacks accordingly.

Platform

A console release is planned for PS4 & PS5 with an emphasis on high graphics quality, making it unsuitable for the Switch. Xbox is a potential option, but the preference is for PS4 & PS5 exclusivity.

Genre

Sheer Silent is a single-player game combining Stealth, Action, RPG, and Story elements. The player infiltrates enemy territory and eliminates foes, with a slow pace that punishes mistakes and makes every encounter unique, with enemies possessing guns, weapons, and communication devices.

Theme

Sheer Silent is a dark, detective-style game with a color palette to match. The player must utilize the darkness to their advantage and find enemies in a city setting. The theme is set in modern times, not medieval or futuristic.

Target Audiance

Sheer Silent targets male gamers aged 16 and above who are interested in hardcore gaming, single player experiences, and tactical combat. It appeals to fans of stealth games and comic books, as well as players who value gameplay over plot. The objective is to achieve a high kill streak while avoiding detection and taking minimal damage.

Story

The story starts just like another, and we meet the main character/video game protagonist/ player, as we get a brief introduction, his name is Michael conners, he was a professional mixed martial artist who just retired. He has a family and is happy, but suddenly his family gets kidnapped, and he must rescue them. But the organization which captured them is a mysterious one. Where he must use his power to protect his family and rescue them from danger. The story would continue like that as he tracks the enemies down whilst locating multiple bases and infiltrating them to gather information and access to more facilities. After finally tracking down his family he sees his wife and daughter as they are about to be executed, but there is only one enemy left. The head of the organization himself. As a boss battle starts you fight in the middle of a warehouse, jumping over boxes, getting in positions, and getting into melee combat with the final enemy. After Defeating the final boss, you reunite with the family that were being kept in a different room. And it is one big successful conclusion where you find your family.

Setting

The setting is present day earth, location: United states of America, big city. I do not know the exact city, but it does not matter.

Resources

The game features a health bar and 2 upgradeable items: cloaks and gauntlets. Cloaks have various effects such as increased movement speed, longer notice time, or reduced damage taken. Gauntlets also have various effects such as increased damage dealt, ability stamina, or activation speed. Ability stamina is a bar that shows the energy remaining for using abilities and regenerates over time. The game controls include moving, looking, attacking, and menu access with buttons such as L2 for sprint and X for future sight, which allows players to see enemy movements ahead.

Inspirations

Inspiration 1: SuperHot

Quantum Vision and superhot are both great inspirations that I had when first thinking about the concept of this game. I like the slow-motion effects in both games and time distortion, but I decided to change it a bit and alter it. To instead show everyone a bit slower whilst also predicting their next path. This is something I have not seen a lot in games when researching to find other examples for my project but suddenly found out It was not done much. And thus, decided to make this concept of mine.

Inspiration 2: Quantum Break

But also, the spiderman games, it showed me what has already been done with this type of mechanic and I decided to use that as a reference and try to go into a different direction to try and locate a unique selling point. And I did find the answer after some brainstorming.




MDA

Mechanics

1. When hiding behind obstacles sneaking gains a speed boost of 15%

2. When you sneak after sprinting you slide making it where players can get a better movement idea than just sneaking.

Dynamics

1. making it where players will try to move faster and get behind cover more.

2. Making players want to try to sprint before sliding, giving the player a more aggressive point.
