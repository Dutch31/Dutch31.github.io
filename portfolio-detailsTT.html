The Tower Page

This is a 3D roguelike tower-climber game made in Unity, where the player fights through room after room of bosses while leveling up and improving their stats to survive.


Stat Tree

The stat tree is randomly generated using a seed each time the player starts a new run. The system features multiple pages that are also randomly generated based on this seed.

Stat Tree System

The stat tree system tracks which stats are purchased with points earned after defeating each boss. Players can upgrade various randomly placed stats throughout the tree.



Player Stat Tracker

The playerâ€™s stats included health, attack, defense, water damage, acid damage, fire damage, speed, physical damage, and dash power. These stats are tracked and increase over time through the stat tree.

End Boss Power up

At the end of each boss fight, the player chooses between two new weapons or keeping their current one. They also receive five stat tree points to spend on upgrades.

Home Base

The home base serves as a place to recover after a tough fight and to reenter the tower when the player is ready.

Boss Battles

Boss battles take place in premade arenas that showcase a single boss with different attack patterns, ready to punish the player for any misstep.
