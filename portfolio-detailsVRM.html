VR Meliora Page

This page is to show my internship and everything I did and made whilst working there. I joined a company called Saasen Meliora for my internship for 6 months, where i made a lot of progress for them like the NPC behavior, smoke behavior, fire behavior, the environment, all menus, and more.


NPCs

The NPCs were made to act as much like normal people as possible, I was tasked to make the behavior activate when there was a fire. The NPCs would then nead to head to a gathering zone and wait for the player to make sure everyone was there.

Working at Meliora

I grew a lot whilst working at my internship as I really learned the structure and method for creating an end product which cant really be teached. I learned the importance of meetings, taking notes, documentation, and how to work effectively.



Working in VR

This was the second time working in VR but the first time I had to work whilst building to a headset, so I basically didnt have a lot of memory to work with. Trying to make it so that the headset didnt lag was a lot of effort.
