VR Shark Cage Page

This project was made with 1 other developer and 1 artist. we were given the task to make an experience within a 1x1x1 cube. so we thought the shark cage could be our cube and have the experience happen around it in vr. so what happens in the game is simple. you enter vr and get to choose how deep you go. after 1 minute or so a shark spawns and rams the cage to give a little scare.

Experiance

The experience was good and we decided to try and make the envirenment look as alive as possible making it feel like you were really there. we focused a lot on finding good audio to immerse the player as well.

Art

The art was made by our artist and i think it looks really good and added a lot to the project.

VR

The choice of making it a vr experience helped us a lot and was a really good idea. It made the experience so much more immersive for the player than just looking at a screen.
